Case Study

Real work. Real results.

A deep dive into how I helped Ogaei — a Canadian virtual healthcare clinic — grow conversions through Google Ads PMax.

🏥
Ogaei
Ontario Virtual Healthcare Clinic — Canada's telehealth platform connecting patients with licensed doctors online
Google Ads Performance Max Healthcare Creative Production
The Challenge
Breaking into a competitive, regulated market
Healthcare advertising in Canada is one of the toughest verticals — heavily regulated, high CPCs, and audiences that require trust before converting. Ogaei needed to drive online consultation bookings efficiently, without burning budget on unqualified clicks.
CPA Spike to $133.83
August 2025: after conversion tracking was corrected, real CPA was exposed — $133.83/conv with only 25 conversions that month
Conversion Tracking Misconfiguration
Early months (Jun–Jul 2025) showed inflated conversion counts — all GA4 events were being counted, not just Lead_Finish_Registration
Low CTR at 2.11%
July 2025: 129K impressions but only 2,275 clicks — assets were not resonating, low ad relevance signals
Meta Conversion Block
Meta's healthcare policy blocked pixel-based conversion tracking — Google Ads PMax became the sole performance channel requiring full optimization
My Role
End-to-end campaign ownership
As the dedicated Google Ads manager, I took full ownership of campaign strategy, creative production, tracking setup, and weekly reporting. I was also responsible for the Meta presence — organic content, ad creative, and page management.
GA4 + Google Ads Tracking Audit
Rebuilt conversion tracking end-to-end — booking confirmations, form submits, and phone call events
PMax Campaign Architecture
Built segmented asset groups by service type (general care, mental health, prescriptions) with custom audience signals
Creative Production
Produced 20+ branded ad assets — static images, lifestyle photography, headline variants, and responsive display ads
Weekly Reporting Cadence
Delivered branded weekly performance decks with actionable recommendations and trend analysis
Strategy Breakdown
How I approached the PMax build
Step 01
Tracking Foundation
Fixed GA4 → Google Ads linking. Set primary conversion: booking confirmation page. Secondary: phone calls + form submissions. Verified via Tag Assistant.
Step 02
Audience Signals
Built custom intent audiences targeting Ontario-based users searching for "virtual doctor", "online prescription", and healthcare condition keywords. Layered with remarketing lists.
Step 03
Asset Group Segmentation
Split into 3 asset groups: General Care, Mental Health, and Chronic Conditions. Each with tailored headlines, descriptions, and creatives — no one-size-fits-all.
Step 04
Creative Testing
Launched with 5 headline variants per group. Killed underperformers weekly. Tested lifestyle imagery vs. clinical look — lifestyle won by 2.4x CTR.
Step 05
Bid Strategy Tuning
Started on Maximize Conversions, then shifted to Target CPA once we had 30+ conversions/month. Adjusted target incrementally to push volume without sacrificing quality.
Step 06
Weekly Optimization Loop
Monday audit: asset ratings, search themes, budget pacing. Midweek check: conversion anomalies. Friday report delivered to client with next week's plan.

Ovasave
UAE Women's Fertility & Hormonal Health Brand — supplements and at-home hormone test kits sold across UAE and Saudi Arabia
Google Ads PMax + Search + Shopping UAE · KSA EN + AR
The Challenge
Two markets, two languages, two product lines
Ovasave operates across UAE and Saudi Arabia, selling both supplements (Amazon.ae only) and at-home fertility/hormone test kits (noon + direct). The challenge: run profitable campaigns in a sensitive health vertical across two GCC markets simultaneously — in both English and Arabic — with distinct audiences, different purchase intent signals, and no unified tracking baseline.
Multi-Market Complexity
UAE and KSA audiences behave differently — different search behaviour, different sensitivity to health messaging, different competitive landscape
Bilingual Campaign Architecture
Required separate campaign sets in English and Arabic — creative, copy, and landing page alignment for both languages
Two Product Categories
Supplements and test kits have different funnel depths — supplements are repeat purchases, test kits are single-event buys
Channel Fragmentation
Sales split between Google Ads direct, Amazon.ae, and noon UAE — attribution required careful UTM architecture across platforms
Approach
30+ campaigns, 2 markets, full-funnel build
Built and managed a full campaign architecture covering Performance Max, Search, and Shopping — split by market (UAE/KSA), language (EN/AR), and product line (supplements/test kits). Ran continuous A/B testing on creative and copy with quarterly refresh cycles.
PMax Sales Campaigns (EN + AR)
Built separate PMax asset groups per language with localised headlines, images, and audience signals for each market
Search Campaigns — Fertility & Hormones
Intent-based search campaigns targeting high-value queries around AMH testing, fertility supplements, and hormone health
Shopping & PMax Feed-Only
Product feed campaigns for supplements and test kits — optimised product titles, descriptions, and pricing for UAE market
KSA Expansion
Launched dedicated Saudi campaigns: Hormone Test, Fertility Test, AMH Test — both EN and AR — to tap a high-intent GCC market
